App-Raspberry Pi Communication (via BLE)​



As it can be seen, in the video above, we were able to achieve basic communication between the reader module (constituted by the Raspberry Pi and AS3992 RFID reader) and a WebApp, creating modular reading and writing functions in both devices.

This will be useful to communicate between devices, sending the necessary messages, for example, the tags read.
